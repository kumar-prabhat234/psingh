1. Darjeeling

Darjeeling is located in West Bengal. The climate is so good to stay throught the summer but has a bit more colder during winter. The few places that I found nice is: Rock garden, Tea garden, Tenzing Climbing Rock, Tiger Hills and so on. You can see nice view of Mt. Kanchanjunga from Tiger Hills. So it is good to visit Tiger Hills on clear day. The only one steam engine train that runs in India is in Darjeeling only. It runs from Darjeeling to Siliguri which is a journey of 6 hours. Toy train is also very famous in Darjeeling.

2. Nainital

Nainital is located in Dehradun and is 345 km (214 mi) from New Delhi. Nainital has full of greenary and is a hillstation which is 6,837 ft above sea level. The places which are good one for tourist are: Nanital Lake, Snow View Point, Tiffin to and Eco cave garden. Eco cave garden is awesome. You will pass through very small gaps in the cave which is thrilling one. Naini lake is good for just staying looking at the lake and enjoying the fresh air. To view different ranges of Himalayas you can visit to snow view point.

3. Mussoorie

Mussoorie is a hill station which is located in Dehradun district which is about 35 kilometres from Dehradun city. The few interesting place to look out in mussoorie are Kempty water falls and robber's cave. Kempty water falls is good place to explore. Paragliding can also be done in mussoorie.Mussoorie is at an average altitude of 1,880 metres (6,170 ft).
